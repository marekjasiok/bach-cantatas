About

Welcome to Das Kantatenwerk! Our purpose is to take you through the Lutheran liturgical year to discover — or rediscover — the entirety of Bach's extant corpus of church cantatas.

By far, the best way to experience the Kantatenwerk is to listen to them on the very day they were composed for, following the rhythm of the church calendar as Bach himself intended.

How it works

hearing

Listen — Follow the liturgical calendar. When you've listened to a cantata, tap the ear icon.

library_music

Explore — Explore the full catalogue by cycle, occasion, key, or BWV number.

favorite

Like — If you enjoyed a cantata, tap the heart. Your favourites are saved here.

hub

Discover — Based on what you like, we recommend cantatas with similar features.

Passions & Oratorios

As a bonus, and a listening challenge, we've also included the Passions and Oratorios, as they share many similarities in form and complete the liturgical calendar. These major works appear with a distinct card throughout the app.
